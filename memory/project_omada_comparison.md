**What Omada is:** All-in-one AI marketing platform for consumer-facing local SMBs (restaurants, retail, salons). ~$270/month (~$9/day). 8 AI "specialists" coordinated by an AI named Alpha: Social Media Manager, Graphics Designer, Video Producer, Local Presence Manager, Chat Agent, SMS Specialist (coming soon), Business Analyst, Campaign Strategist. Organic growth only — no paid media.

**Program 1 overlap (moderate):** Omada's Social Manager + Graphics Designer overlap with the Content Adapter concept, but Omada generates content autonomously without human input. myNHD's Program 1 is human-in-the-loop by design (Laura Kressig supplies content briefs). Omada lacks LinkedIn support. Omada costs ~$270/mo all-in vs. myNHD's ~$150/mo estimated tool costs (using existing licensed tools).

**Program 2 overlap (none):** Omada has no CoStar integration, no lead processor, no Levitate/CRM integration, no B2B outbound prospecting capability. Its Chat Agent handles inbound; myNHD Program 2 is outbound commercial real estate prospecting — a completely different use case. SMS Specialist (coming soon) is retention-focused, not B2B prospecting.

**Fundamental positioning gap:** Omada is built for consumer-facing local SMBs. myNHD is a B2B firm marketing to real estate agents and brokers. Autonomous content generation without human review is a liability for a professional B2B audience.

**Why:** Researched after proposals were drafted to validate positioning and pricing.

**How to apply:** If myNHD or Steve Schattmaier raises a competitor like Omada, use this to differentiate — myNHD's Programs use existing licensed tools, are human-in-the-loop, and address a B2B use case that Omada doesn't serve.
