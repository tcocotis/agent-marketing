Always adapt tone to match the content being created.

**Why:** User explicitly chose "match the context" — they want the right feel for each piece, not a one-size-fits-all voice.

**How to apply:**
- Reports, briefs, strategy docs → professional and concise
- Emails, social content → friendly and conversational
- Ad copy, headlines, campaign concepts → bold and creative
- Always clear — no filler or jargon regardless of tone
